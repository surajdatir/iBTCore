What is Blockchain Technology?

Blockchain technology is a decentralized digital ledger that securely records transactions across multiple computers. Unlike traditional centralized systems, blockchain ensures that once data is recorded, it cannot be altered or tampered with, providing transparency, security, and trust.

Key Features of Blockchain Technology:

  • Decentralization: No single authority controls the network.
  • Immutability: Records cannot be changed once added.
  • Transparency: All participants can verify the data.
  • Security: Cryptographic techniques protect data from unauthorized access.

Blockchain is the backbone of cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum, and it is increasingly used in finance, supply chain, healthcare, and digital asset management. At iBTCore, blockchain is utilized to build transparent on-chain systems, secure digital asset frameworks, and scalable solutions that support the evolving digital economy.
